the reDirect journey

We created reDirect as part of our submission to Hack Western 9, Western University's annual hackathon.

With Hack Western 9 being the first hackathon to be back in-person after three years, we realized that there has been a surge of people who have started to work from home. Now, don't get us wrong, there are plenty of reasons why someone might willingly choose to work from home. They no longer have to wake up earlier to transit to work. They get to work from the convenience of their home. However, problems have also risen from this new experience for some, including a lack of organizational systems when it comes to at-home working.

reDirect was created to act as an all-in-one customizable hub that allows users to conveniently open up their most used tabs for work. Users can use our preset links or customize their hub to be their own.

You may be wondering, "What's so revolutonary about reDirect?" Well, the magic happens behind the screen. With the help of machine intelligence and machine learning, reDirect can read how many fingers you hold up and send you to the page you want. Technology is crazy these days, isn't it?'
All you have to do is leave your webcam on and while the reDirect tab is open, it can open up whatever tab you like as soon as you hold up the appropriate amount of fingers.

We hope you enjoy and behefit from reDirect, and learn something new as well! We had a blast creating this project together and can't wait to further develop it in the near future.

- The reDirect Team
